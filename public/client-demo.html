System Workflow

1. Collect Scrapes YouTube videos and Shorts from the latest monitoring window using Vijay, TVK, Thalapathy, politics, CM, and speech keywords.
2. Store Writes every link into Google Sheets with video type, channel, views, likes, comments, and description.
3. Analyze Uses captions/transcripts when available; otherwise analyzes title, channel, and description to score sentiment.
4. Prioritize Highlights negative or high-risk videos with direct YouTube links for PR/legal follow-up.
